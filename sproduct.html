The Classic Leather Jacket is a cornerstone piece of any refined wardrobe. Handcrafted from premium full-grain Italian leather, each jacket is individually cut and sewn by skilled artisans in our partner atelier.

The buttery-soft leather develops a beautiful patina over time, making each piece uniquely yours. Features include a fully lined satin interior for smooth layering, antique brass YKK zippers, dual interior pockets, and adjustable waist tabs for a tailored silhouette.

Whether paired with dark denim for a casual evening or draped over a dress for a night out, this jacket transitions effortlessly between occasions, embodying the LUXE philosophy of timeless, versatile luxury.
